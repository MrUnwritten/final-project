About Me

Hey there, my name is Victor, glad you stumbled on by. Where to start? Oh yeah obviously gaming.
As a kid, gaming was all I had because my dad always told me it was homework first then I could play games but could never go out or have friends because that was a distraction.
So for a while, I was always top of my class but then I started realizing I wasn't as happy as people thought I was especially having tons of games.
And to break out of that I came up with a loop hole in my dads rules if I can't go out and have friends why not make friends on games.
Immediately went out and tried earning money for a cheap gaming headset by selling my snacks/sodas, mowing lawns, and even cleaning cars.
And when I got my first ever headset I felt like I was above the world. Making friends left and right on games I loved.
After a while my dad saw that I was happier and still keeping my grades up and changed his mind.
But I knew at that moment in life gaming was going to be a huge part of my life.
And if I could give anyone any type of advice it would be don't let anyone stop you from doing the things you love!
Stay Positive, look for solutions, and never give up no matter how many times you fall just get up and push forward!
